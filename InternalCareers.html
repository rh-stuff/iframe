TRANSFERS AND PROMOTIONS

RH is committed to hiring and promoting the “right” people into positions that are professionally challenging and personally enriching. Transfers and promotions are outward displays of RH’s commitment to these goals.

In general, to be eligible for a transfer or promotion, you must be in your current position for a minimum of twelve (12) months and be in “good standing.” Good standing means that you must have received a minimum of a “Meets Expectations” rating on your most recent performance review, and not be on any current disciplinary actions.

APPROVAL FOR CHANGING ROLES

If you are interested in a transfer, you must obtain approval from your current leader to apply for a role. Once approved, contact the leader, and/or recruiter, in the new location or department to discuss a role on their team. We also request that you apply online for the role and complete the "Transfer Eligibility" section on the internal application.

RELOCATION ASSISTANCE

RH will provide relocation assistance for associates, when appropriate, for opportunities within RH. Further details on this program will be discussed upon extension of an offer.
